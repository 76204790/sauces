Sauces

Pineapple

A very spicy, layered habanero and pineapple sauce that complements fish/chicken tacos. Colour and intensity may vary depending on batch of habaneros that are in season.

Ingredients:
  1. Habenero Peppers
  2. Cilantro
  3. Garlic Cloves
  4. White Vinegar
  5. Water
  6. Lime Juice
  7. Salt
  8. Sliced Peaches
  9. Honey
  10. Pineapple

Jalapeno Corriander

A mild and smokey roasted jalapeno sauce with coriander.

Ingredients:
  1. Jalapeno Peppers
  2. Onions
  3. Garlic CLoves
  4. White Vinegar
  5. Water
  6. Lime Juice
  7. Salt
  8. Mexican Oregano
  9. Cumin
  10. Cilantro Sprigs
  11. Black Pepper
